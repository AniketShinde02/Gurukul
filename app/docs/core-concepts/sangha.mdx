# The Sangha System

In Sanskrit, **Sangha** means "community" or "assembly". In Gurukul, a Sangha is a dedicated space for specific study groups.

### Key Components

- **Public Rooms**: Open to all students.
- **Private Sanghas**: Require an invitation or specific role.
- **Roles**: Admin, Moderator, Member.

### Benefits of Sangha

- **Focused Collaboration**: Study only with people who share your goals.
- **Shared Whiteboards**: Every session in a Sangha room provides collaborative tools.
- **Persistence**: Messages and resources stay within the Sangha.
