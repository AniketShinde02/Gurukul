# How to Create a Sangha

Follow these steps to establish your own study community on Gurukul.

## Step 1: Navigate to Sangha Page
Click on the **Sangha** link in the navigation header.

## Step 2: Click "Create Sangha"
Look for the plus (+) button or "Create Sangha" trigger on the dashboard.

## Step 3: Define Your Community
Fill in the details:
- **Name**: Choose a clear, student-focused name (e.g., "UPSC Mains preparation").
- **Description**: What is the goal of this Sangha?
- **Privacy**: Decide if it should be public or private.

## Step 4: Customize Rules
Invite your first members and set the community rules based on the **Community Guidelines**.
