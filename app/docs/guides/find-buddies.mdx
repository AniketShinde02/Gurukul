# How to Find Study Buddies

Matchmaking is the fastest way to start a session.

### Steps

1. **Go to Dashboard**: Access the main home or rooms page.
2. **Select Mode**: Choose between "Global" (anyone) or "Buddies" (existing contacts).
3. **Start Search**: Click the "Find Partner" button.
4. **Connect**: Once a match is found, your video and audio will automatically connect.

### Pro Tips for Better Matches

- **Update Your Profile**: Users with complete profiles are more likely to be accepted as long-term buddies.
- **Stay Active**: Be ready to engage as soon as the match connects.
- **Respect Privacy**: Use the session for studying, and report any inappropriate behavior immediately.
